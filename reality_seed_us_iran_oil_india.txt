PART II — INDIA-SPECIFIC EXPOSURE (SEED FACTS)
================================================================

A. Energy Dependency
- India imports ~87% of its crude oil.
- ~45% of India's crude imports transit the Strait of Hormuz (Saudi Arabia, Iraq, UAE, Kuwait).
- Russia accounts for ~35% of India's crude (post-2022 shift), primarily ESPO and Urals grades — not Hormuz-dependent but subject to G7 price cap and secondary sanctions.
- Strategic Petroleum Reserve (SPR): 39.2 million barrels — covers roughly 9.5 days of net imports.
- Diesel and petrol retail prices frozen by OMCs (IOCL, BPCL, HPCL) since 2024 — under-recovery now estimated at ₹18/litre on diesel.

B. Macroeconomic Linkages
- Every $10/bbl sustained rise in crude adds ~$15 billion to India's import bill annually and ~30 bps to CPI.
- Current account deficit projection revised from 1.2% to 3.4% of GDP if $130/bbl holds for two quarters.
- INR under pressure: depreciated from 83.4 to 87.9 per USD in 10 days. RBI intervention drawing down forex reserves (now $612B from $648B).
- Equity markets: Nifty 50 down 8.3% in 8 sessions; aviation, paint, tyre, and logistics stocks hardest hit.

C. Diplomatic Position
- India maintains strategic relationships with both the US (QUAD, iCET, defense pacts) and Iran (Chabahar Port, INSTC corridor, historical ties).
- India abstained from the UNSC resolution condemning Iran's Hormuz closure (April 17, 2026).
- External Affairs Minister held calls with US Secretary of State, Iranian Foreign Minister, and Saudi/UAE counterparts within 72 hours of the Hormuz closure.
- Approximately 9 million Indian diaspora in the Gulf (Saudi, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain) — source of ~$55 billion in annual remittances.

D. Domestic Political Context
- General election cycle completed May 2024; government has political room but mid-term state elections (Bihar, WB) upcoming.
- Opposition framing: "failure to lock in Russian oil discounts," "surrender of Chabahar leverage."
- Fuel subsidy debate reopening; LPG subsidy extension demands from rural constituencies.
